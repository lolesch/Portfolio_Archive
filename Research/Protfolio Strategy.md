# About Me

A teaser / mini story pitching yourself -> can be followed up with a more detailed version / biography
need to be specific to your strengths, NOT generally applicable to everyone!

- who you are
- what you care about 
	- will narrow down (careful!)
- how you work
- where you're headed
	- should be niche/specific

- write in first person
- authentic and concise
- to the point, no complicated language
- photo humanizes your profile - can work against you
- ~~mention~~ *show* soft skills or unique strengths
- show personality

- don't overuse buzzwords
- don't lie
