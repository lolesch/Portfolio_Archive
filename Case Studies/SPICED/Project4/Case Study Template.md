## Case Study Storytelling
How to structure your case study

My strategy for:
- The Hook
	- convince HR they made the right choice opening my case study
	- highlight the tech background / broad surface profile
	- results first approach
- Research
	- to show you care about research-driven / user-centered solutions
	- Insights are more valuable than screenshots.
- Sketches and wireframing
	- understanding of layout concepts, UI elements, and information hierarchy
	- thought process and journey from idea/research insights to final product
- Prototyping and iteration
	- highlight multi device / responsive design
	- highlight usability testing
	- prototype links
- Outcomes and lessons learned
	- self-reflection and what you have learned
		- show growth and adaptability
