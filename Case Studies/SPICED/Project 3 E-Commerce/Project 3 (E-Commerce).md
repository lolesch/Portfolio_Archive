# Project Content
## Intro

An interactive e-commerce for a niche product with strong focus on design-principles and -system, prototyping and usability testing within 3 weeks

The Idea of highly customizable Roller-Skates emerged from the briefs demand of a niche product. The focus on individualization was deliberately chosen as the basis for variables and modes.

With developer backgrounds we wanted to explore the technical capabilities of Figma’s prototyping and testing the latest feature: Slots.

## Context

- 3-weeks project with high focus on design principles and usability testing
- A Desktop-first product

## My Role

With strong programming background and a heads up experience with Figma, I took leadership on how to 
- set up modes and variables
- apply them to Instance Variants 
- set them in the prototype interactions
Most prototype wiring was made by me.
The editor Idea and early stages
#### NOT my role
the shopping flow (Cart to Confirmation) designs were mainly made by my teammate.

## Key Challenge

Finding the right interaction between variables, modes and where to apply them in nested instance variants costed a lot of time when it came to troubleshooting.
Also micro interactions caused problems with not correctly applying variables due to nested interaction priority.
Another think was the amount of work to set everything up correctly while the project was still going through major design changes. this lead to unnecessary loss of state that was working beforehand.

## Methods

We applied
- design principles
- design system
- usability testing
- accessibility
- self learning
	- high personal goals, variables were not expected

## Learnings

- no support for smart animating variable mode changes.
- issues with 'scroll to' setups when using instance variants
- agile usability testing worked best for us due to fast iteration cycles
- do not rush wireframing
	- this was a flaw from the project setup, where we needed to get to a testable state ASAP
- design-principles and -system are very powerful when applied early on and consistently
